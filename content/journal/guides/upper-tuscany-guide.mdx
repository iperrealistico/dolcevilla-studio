Upper Tuscany is part of the brand because it is not the most predictable version of the region. It is quieter, more private, and often more emotionally textured.

Being near Lucca while also connected to the coast, marble landscapes, and inland countryside creates a wider visual world. For couples, that means more than variety. It means a celebration can feel surprising without losing coherence.
