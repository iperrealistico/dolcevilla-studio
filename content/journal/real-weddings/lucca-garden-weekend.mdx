For this wedding weekend, the story was never just the ceremony. It was the welcome dinner, the pauses between family conversations, and the way evening light settled into the garden before dinner began.

That is where this kind of coverage becomes most meaningful: not in the obvious highlights alone, but in the emotional transitions between them.
